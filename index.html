About Me

I use remote sensing, big data, and machine learning to better understand our environment, find interesting insights, and create solutions for a sustainable future.

Currently, I am studying the use of satellite imagery and watershed characteristics to estimate nutrient levels, including Total Phosphorus, Total Nitrogen, and Total Suspended Solids. Please refer to the PhD section for more details about my work (currently a work in progress).
